📜 Report Context

This interactive dashboard establishes the governance of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for Korn Translations, part of the Idlewild Burg group. The primary goal is to provide the Senior Leadership Team with real-time visibility into the organization's risk posture and control effectiveness. All indicators are aligned with industry best practices, global privacy guidelines (GDPR/LGPD), and the rigorous requirements of the ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standard.

Explore the sections below to visualize consolidated maturity scores, tactical incident response capabilities, and our journey toward a Zero Trust architecture for 2025.

📈 1. Strategic Level (Board View)

The Strategic view translates complex technical data into a single business metric: the Information Security Maturity Index (ISMI). This score summarizes the organization's cybersecurity health, facilitating executive decision-making and investment allocation. Scores below 40% indicate a "Reactive" posture, while those above 80% reflect "Optimized" processes.

🚀 5. 2025 Implementation Roadmap

To consolidate current maturity and align Korn Translations with a Zero Trust architecture, our governance timeline is divided into three progressive phases, followed by 2025's high-priority strategic directives.

Phase 1: Integration Monthly

  • Automation: Weekly DLP reports and Wizer metric integration.
  • Inventory: Real-time asset mapping updates for better ISMI calculation.

Phase 2: Management Review Quarterly

  • Review: ISO 27001 Item 9.3 Management Reviews.
  • Assessment: Re-evaluating risks showing increased severity in the matrix.

Phase 3: Continuous Improvement Continuous

  • Training: Triggered campaigns if Engagement Index (SEI) drops below 90%.
  • Resilience: Review Contingency Plans (C001-C017) if MTTR lags.

2025 Strategic Directives (Zero Trust Framework)

1. Mandatory Universal MFA

Enforce Multi-Factor Authentication for 100% of accounts. VPN, AWS, and Google Workspace must require MFA, establishing Identity as the new perimeter.

2. Network & Data Micro-segmentation

Restricting access to client data (Asset AI08) strictly to Project/Production roles via Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and least-privilege principles.

3. AI Homologation (Plan M027)

Block unsanctioned public Generative AI tools. Standardize use of Enterprise/API-driven AI to prevent inadvertent data leakage during translation tasks.
